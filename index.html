Recipe #1: Sabudana Kichdi

  • Time: 50min
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity Price
Sabudana100g10 euros
Potatoes31 euro
Coriander leaves10g1 euro

Step 1: Wash and soak sabodana

Step 2: Grind 1/2 of pea nuts.

Step 3: Boil potatoes and heat oil

Step 4: Add soaked sabodana salt and chilli

Step 5: Mix well add fried peanuts

Step 6: Mix and cook until sabodana turns transparent.

Step 7: Garnish with corriander and lemon
